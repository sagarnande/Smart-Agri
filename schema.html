Why the Government Schema is necessary to farmers :

Government schemes for agriculture provide financial aid, subsidies, crop insurance, and access to modern technology. They support irrigation, storage, and transport infrastructure while promoting sustainable and organic farming. These schemes boost rural employment, improve productivity, and ensure food security, helping farmers increase their income and contribute to the economy.

Schemes

Providing the best schemes for farmers

01

National Mission on Natural Farming (NMNF)

This mission promotes sustainable agriculture practices by providing financial assistance of ₹12,200 per hectare over three years. The funds support cluster formation, capacity building, and continuous guidance by trained personnel, aiming to cover 12 lakh hectares across various states.

02

Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)

AIF offers medium to long-term debt financing facilities for investment in viable projects for post-harvest management infrastructure and community farming assets. This initiative aims to improve agricultural infrastructure and supply chains.

Click here for apply
03

Agri-Clinics and Agri-Business Centres (ACABC) Scheme

This scheme aims to supplement the efforts of public extension by providing extension and other services to farmers on payment basis or free of cost as per business model of agri-preneur, local needs and affordability of target group of farmers.

04

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

The Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Scheme is an initiative by the Government of India that provides farmers with timely and affordable credit for agricultural and allied activities. Introduced by the NABARD (National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development) in 1998,

05

Rashtriya Krishi Vikas Yojana (RKVY)

Initiated in 2007, RKVY is an umbrella scheme aimed at ensuring holistic development of agriculture and allied sectors. It incentivizes states to increase public investment in agriculture, promoting growth and sustainability in the sector.

Click here for apply
06

PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana)

PMFBY (Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana) is a crop insurance scheme that provides financial protection to farmers against crop failure due to natural disasters, pests, or diseases. The scheme covers all food and cash crops. It offers low premiums with high coverage, making it affordable for farmers.
